PROGRAM OVERVIEW

Seasonal relief with year-round community support.

Anwaar Foundation programs combine urgent food and welfare assistance with long-term empowerment pathways for vulnerable households.

Our approach is community-centered, transparent, and aligned with dignity-first humanitarian values in Nairobi and Tana River.

PROGRAM HIGHLIGHTS

Our key humanitarian interventions

Each program is designed to deliver immediate support while reinforcing long-term resilience.

Ramadhan

Ramadhan Cooked Meals

Daily iftar distribution in informal settlements to support fasting families with nutritious meals.

  • Community kitchens and volunteer-led delivery
  • Targeted household outreach during fasting month

Eid

Eid Support Programs

Festive meal and meat distribution to ensure vulnerable families and children homes celebrate with dignity.

  • Eid-ul-Fitr and Eid-ul-Adha focused interventions
  • Support for widows, orphans, and households in need

Dry Food Distribution
