ELIGIBILITY GUIDELINES

Who can get involved

Basic requirements help us maintain safe, consistent, and respectful community engagement.

  • Volunteers should be at least 18 years old for independent field activities. Younger participants may join approved supervised events.
  • All applicants should provide accurate contact details and reliable availability information.
  • Volunteers and partners must commit to safeguarding, non-discrimination, and community dignity standards.
  • Institutional partners should have clear points of contact and operational capacity for agreed deliverables.
  • Specialized roles may require prior experience, references, or additional orientation before deployment.
FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Quick answers before you apply

How long does it take to receive a response after submitting?

Most inquiries are reviewed within 3 to 7 business days. During major campaigns, response time may be slightly longer.

Can I volunteer on weekends only?

Yes. Indicate your preferred days and hours in the volunteer form and we will match you with suitable schedules.

Do you accept one-time volunteers and short-term support?

Yes, we welcome both one-time and recurring participation depending on active program needs.

What kinds of organizations can partner with Anwaar Foundation?

Companies, NGOs, schools, faith institutions, CBOs, and donor groups can partner through co-funded campaigns, in-kind support, or service collaboration.

Can we support from outside Nairobi or outside Kenya?

Absolutely. Remote support is possible through sponsorship, digital advocacy, fundraising, and strategic technical assistance.
