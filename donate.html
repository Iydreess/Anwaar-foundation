How to Donate via M-Pesa Paybill

  1. Open M-Pesa on your phone.
  2. Select Lipa na M-Pesa, then Pay Bill.
  3. Enter Business Number: 985050.
  4. For Account Number, enter 0800087002.
  5. Enter the donation amount and your M-Pesa PIN.
  6. Confirm details and complete payment.

After donating, please share your confirmation message through our contact page for acknowledgment and receipt support.

Giving FAQ

Can I donate from outside Kenya?

Yes. We welcome international donors through secure digital payment channels and partner-supported remittance options.

Will I receive donation confirmation?

Yes. You receive confirmation after successful processing, and additional documentation can be requested through our contact team.

Can I dedicate my donation to a specific program?

Yes. Share your preferred focus area and our team will guide your contribution to the right campaign stream.
