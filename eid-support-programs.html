Eid Support Programs

Programs / Eid Support Programs

PROGRAM DETAIL

Celebrations with dignity for vulnerable households.

Our Eid interventions provide festive meals and meat support during Eid-ul-Fitr and Eid-ul-Adha.

We prioritize low-income families, widows, orphans, and children homes so celebrations remain inclusive and dignified.
